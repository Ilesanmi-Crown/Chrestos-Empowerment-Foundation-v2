Overview

CHRESTHOS Empowerment Foundation is a dedicated nonprofit organization committed to transforming lives and communities through targeted, compassionate interventions. We focus on unlocking the potential of vulnerable populations, particularly children and young individuals, by providing comprehensive support, educational resources, and sustainable community development solutions.

He serves as a Trustee of the LODO Foundation, an organization focused on charitable outreach and empowerment. He is also the Patron of the Owerri Women’s Association, UK, where he supports cultural preservation and the welfare of women in the diaspora.Tee served with distinction as President of the Rotary Club of Hendon, London, where he led various philanthropic initiatives and community service projects.
