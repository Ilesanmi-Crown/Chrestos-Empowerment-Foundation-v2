The Next Teen Stars

Adolescence is a time of self-discovery and identity formation, when young people begin to establish a sense of who they are, what they believe in, and where they fit in the world. This makes adolescence a sensitive developmental window, characterized by significant opportunities. The better we help this young ones find natural talents, the more they can draw from them to search for and identify opportunities that will give them the chance to do what they do best every day, so they can then turn their raw talents into matured strengths and live up to their full potential....thus the creation of the TEEN SINGING COMPETITION "THE NEXT TEEN STAR"

THE PROJECT

The Next Teen Star is created mainly to mainly to encourage teenagers to learn to express their feelings and ideas and ideas through music. The first stage is the auditions where all the contestants are expected to send 2-3 minutes video entries of their songs. Second stage, the videos would be scrutinized by seasoned music acts, and winners selected. This winners will undergo training for the allocated period of time and would subsequently be given an opportunity to record their songs in a recording studio which would be sponsored by the organizers of " The Next Teen Star"
